Installation Notes

Use professional installation and waterproof handling.

Cut correctly

Cut only at marked positions and keep the cut flat and clean before sealing.

Power off

Always disconnect power during installation, maintenance and wiring.

Avoid twisting

Do not twist, swing, drag, right-angle bend or repeatedly bend the strip.

Match power

Confirm voltage, current and power adapter match the strip workload.

Waterproof terminals

Handle terminals and connectors with effective waterproof and anti-corrosion treatment.

Professional work

Installation, repair and maintenance should be completed by qualified professionals.
